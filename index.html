What makes TimeLot different

01 —

A formula, not a feeling

Every activity gets a priority score computed from importance, deadline, duration, flexibility, and how much is already done. You see the number. You understand why.

02 —

Nothing moves without your approval

TimeLot proposes. You confirm. No silent calendar rewrites, no surprises. You stay in control of every committed hour.

03 —

Built for your whole life

Work. Family. Personal. Sleep. TimeLot understands that your 24 hours aren't all the same — and schedules accordingly, within the windows that belong to each part of your life.

04 —

Completion changes everything

As you make progress, the engine re-ranks in real time. Nearly finished tasks naturally deprioritize. What's urgent surfaces. What's done steps aside.

05 —

Your calendar. Your data.

TimeLot connects to Google Calendar and Outlook to read your existing commitments — and write new ones only when you say so. Privacy by design, not afterthought.

06 —

Available wherever you are

iPhone. Android. Desktop. Web. One engine, every device. Your priority list is always one tap away — not waiting at your desk.
